Getting Here
🚤

From White Beach: Take a tricycle or habal-habal heading towards Tabinay. Approximately 10–15 minutes ride. Look for the yellow Kape Ni Lola sign on the left side of the road.

⚓

From the Port (Muelle): Rent a tricycle or habal-habal along Muelle Pier Road. Head towards Barangay Tabinay — about 20 minutes depending on traffic.

🛵

By Habal-Habal: Easily flagged down along the main road. Tell the driver "Kape Ni Lola sa Tabinay" — most locals know us!

🅿 Free Parking Available — We have free parking for up to 10 vehicles in front of the cafe. Motorbike parking is available on the side.
