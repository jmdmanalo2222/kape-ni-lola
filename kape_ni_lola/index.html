Our Story

More Than Coffee.
It's a Feeling.

Kape Ni Lola began with a simple memory — the smell of barako brewing in our grandmother's kitchen every morning in Batangas. We built this cafe to bottle that feeling and share it with everyone who walks through our door.

Every item on our menu honors Filipino pantry traditions while meeting modern cafe expectations. We source our beans directly from Benguet highlands, work with local sugar farmers for our muscovado, and bake our pastries fresh each morning before the first cup is poured.
